Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email, school and class level, as well as usage data like study time, quiz results and AI chat interactions, to personalise your learning experience.

2. How we use your information

We use your data to deliver personalised tutoring, generate progress reports for parents and teachers, process payments, and improve PrepWise's AI models and content.

3. Data protection & compliance

PrepWise handles personal data in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) and applies GDPR-aware practices for international users, including encryption of sensitive data and role-based access control.

4. Children's data

For learners under 18, a parent or guardian account is linked to the child's profile. We collect only the information necessary to deliver the service and never sell children's data to third parties.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited data is shared with payment processors (Paystack, Flutterwave, Stripe), cloud storage providers, and school administrators for students enrolled under an institution account.

6. Data security

We use HTTPS/SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, rate limiting, audit logs and daily backups to protect your data.

7. Your rights

You can access, correct, export or request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting olatundeajayi87@yahoo.com.

8. Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and tools like Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how PrepWise is used and to improve the platform.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice.

AI Personal Study Planner

A revision plan built around your exam date

Tell PrepWise your exam date and weak subjects — it builds a daily, weekly and monthly plan, complete with reminders and Pomodoro sessions.

How it works

Three inputs. One complete revision plan.

  • Set your exam and target date
  • Tell PrepWise your strong and weak subjects
  • Choose how many hours you can study per day

The AI planner then generates a day-by-day countdown, balances revision across subjects, and adjusts automatically as you complete topics or fall behind.

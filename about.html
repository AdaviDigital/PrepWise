Our story

Built in Lagos, for every Nigerian learner

PrepWise exists to close the gap between how Nigerian students are taught and how they are examined — using AI that understands the WAEC, NECO and JAMB syllabus as well as any human tutor.

Our mission

Every learner deserves a tutor who never gets tired of explaining

Millions of Nigerian students prepare for high-stakes exams with outdated textbooks, overcrowded classrooms and limited access to extra lessons. PrepWise was built to put a patient, curriculum-aligned AI tutor and a complete past-question library in every learner's pocket — regardless of their location or income.

We work with teachers, examiners and curriculum specialists to keep every explanation, question and study plan grounded in what Nigerian students are actually examined on.

Our Mission

Make personalised, curriculum-aligned exam preparation accessible to every Nigerian student.

Our Vision

Become Africa's leading AI-powered learning companion, from primary school to professional exams.

Our Values

Accuracy, accessibility, data privacy, and respect for every learner's pace.

Our Community

120,000+ students, 850+ partner schools and thousands of parents and teachers.
